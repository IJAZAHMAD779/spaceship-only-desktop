The origins:

The StarCruiser 5000 is a pioneering spacecraft, designed with a singular mission in mind: to explore the Sun. Built with advanced thermal protection systems and cutting-edge solar technology, this ship is capable of withstanding extreme conditions as it makes its journey toward our solar system's center.

With a highly specialized crew, the StarCruiser 5000 embarked on its first journey to the Sun, a mission that was considered impossible for previous generations of spacecraft. Onboard, scientists & astronauts observed the Sun's outer layers, collected valuable data on solar winds, and even took stunning images of solar flares.
